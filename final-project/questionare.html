Site Planning Questionnaire

Who is the target audience?

Fans of Tyler the Creator and/or people wanting to attend one of his concerts

How can I tailor the web site to reach that audience?

By having a date guide based on show dates and locations as well as theming it for the specific album he is touring for, in this case Call Me If You Get Lost: Estate Sale

What are the goals for the site?

To make a visually appealing site that matches the theme of the album but also gives the appropriate information

How will I gather the information?

Researching tour websites to get an idea of the proper layout and go through the artist's media to understand the color palette and design choices

What are my sources for multimedia content?

YouTube videos/music videos, images, posters, and lists of dates and locations

What is my budget?

Free

How long do I have to complete the project?

Roughly 3 weeks

Who is on my project team?

Just me

How often should the site be updated?

After each show

Who is responsible for updating the site?

Tour Manager
